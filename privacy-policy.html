Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Introduction

At Baptist Church Onitiri, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website and online services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Contact information (e.g., name, email address) when you reach out to us.
  • Voluntary submissions through forms such as donations, event registrations, or prayer requests.
  • Analytics data like your browser type, device, and general location to improve our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries or prayer requests.
  • Send newsletters, event notifications, and updates.
  • Improve the user experience on our website.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us analyze website traffic and personalize content. You can choose to accept or reject cookies via the pop-up.

5. Third-Party Services

We may embed videos (e.g., Facebook, YouTube), and these platforms may use their own tracking tools. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies separately.

6. Data Security

We implement basic security measures to protect your data, but no online transmission is 100% secure. Always exercise caution when sharing sensitive information.

7. Your Consent

By using our website, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. If you do not agree, you should discontinue use of the site.

8. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page with a new "last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

For questions about this policy, reach out to us at info@bconitiri.org.
